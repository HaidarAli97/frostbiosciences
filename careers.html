Support our scientific research initiatives by conducting experiments, collecting data, and assisting senior scientists in cell imaging research projects.

  • Bachelor’s degree in Biology, Biotechnology, or related field

  • Strong organizational and multitasking skills

Ensure the quality and integrity of our cell imaging products through meticulous testing, analysis, and documentation following industry standards and regulations.

Data Entry Clerk
